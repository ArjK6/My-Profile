Hi, I'm Arj

S3918010

I’m Arj, I’m 28. I am originally from India having immigrated to Australia in 2002. I spent my primary school years in Alice Springs after which our family moved to Hobart and subsequently Launceston which is where my parents and sister reside now. My parents are both doctors so naturally once I finished my schooling I moved to Melbourne to commence my MBBS degree at Monash Uni in 2011. Over time I came to realise that my passions did not lie solely within medicine as I harboured a great interest in computers and technology. Thus I enrolled at RMIT pursuing a degree in IT. I enjoy sports, fitness and gaming. I am quite proficient at racket sports but I did the logical thing and am now trying to get better with sports utilising feet primarily. No structural imbalances here. I speak 4 languages but 3 of them are languages only found in India, have also been learning Spanish for the past 2 years but made muy poco progress. I am a keen Liverpool supporter and I play a fair amount of FPS and Dota 2 on the side.
